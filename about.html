About Redline Motors

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect automotive enthusiasts around the world with the unique parts market in Japan. We believe that distance, language, and complicated logistics should never stop anyone from building the car they dream of. Our passion for racing and motorsports drives everything we do, and we are committed to making the entire process smooth and transparent. From sourcing to inspection to safe international delivery, we work hard to give every customer a reliable and stress free experience.

Our Story

Redline Motors is an American owned JDM export company based in Tokyo. We founded the business in 2025 after years of struggling to access authentic Japanese parts while living and working around the automotive scene here in Japan. As lifelong car enthusiasts with a background as United States Marine Corps veterans, we learned the importance of attention to detail, trust, and accountability. In the beginning we were simply helping friends and other enthusiasts track down rare parts that were difficult to find overseas. That small effort eventually grew into a full export service built on consistent communication, accurate sourcing, and genuine enthusiasm for the automotive community. Today we help customers around the world secure everything from OEM replacements to rare performance parts, wheels, aero kits, and specialty components.
