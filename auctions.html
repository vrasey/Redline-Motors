Yahoo! Auctions Step-by-Step Guide

New to buying from Japan? Here is exactly how to find the perfect part.

1. Translate the Site

Use Google Chrome or Edge. Right-click anywhere on the page and select "Translate to English" to read item details easily.

2. Check Seller Rating

Look for the seller's evaluation score (Sun/Cloud/Rain icons). Avoid sellers with many "Bad" ratings or very low feedback counts.

3. Domestic Shipping

Check if shipping is "Paid by Seller" or "Paid by Winner". Large items (bumpers/engines) can be expensive to ship within Japan.

4. Copy the URL

Once you find the item, simply copy the full web address (URL) from the top of your browser bar.

5. Paste in Form

Paste that link into our quote form. Enter your Max Bid in JPY (the absolute most you'd pay for the item itself).

6. We Handle the Rest

We bid for you. If we win, we handle payment, warehouse inspection, repacking, and international export.
