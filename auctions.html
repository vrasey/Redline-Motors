How It Works: Sourcing Auctions

Follow these steps to secure parts from Yahoo! Auctions, Mercari, or Up Garage.

1. Find Your Part

Browse Japanese sites like Yahoo! Auctions or Croooober. Find the item you want and copy the URL.

2. Paste & Submit

Paste the link below. Enter your Max Bid in JPY (the most you are willing to pay for the item itself).

3. Review Quote

We email you a USD Estimate including shipping. Approve it to proceed to the secure payment portal.

4. We Bid & Ship

Once paid, we bid. If we win, the part is inspected at our Tokyo warehouse and then shipped straight to your driveway.
