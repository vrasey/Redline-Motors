How to Buy a Vehicle

Importing a vehicle from Japan is a detailed process. We manage every step to ensure a smooth and secure transaction.

  1. Step 1: Initial Consultation

    Reach out to us via our contact page with the make, model, and any specific requirements for the vehicle you're looking for. We will discuss your goals and budget.

  2. Step 2: Sourcing & Quote

    Our team will search dealer inventories and private auctions to find matching vehicles. We will provide you with detailed photos, inspection sheets, and a comprehensive quote.

  3. Step 3: Contract & Deposit

    Once you've chosen a vehicle, we will sign a formal sales agreement. A deposit is required to secure the vehicle and begin the export process.

  4. Step 4: Deregistration & Logistics

    We handle all Japanese-side logistics, including purchasing the car, inland transport, and preparing all necessary export and deregistration documents.

  5. Step 5: Shipping & Delivery

    The vehicle is shipped via RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) or container to your nearest port. We provide all documentation needed for you to clear customs in your home country.
