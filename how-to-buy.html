How to Buy

Our process is simple and transparent. Follow these steps to get your parts shipped from Japan directly to you.

  1. Step 1: Find Your Part

    Locate the item you wish to purchase on any Japanese auction or retail site (like Yahoo! Auctions, Up Garage, etc.). Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar.

  2. Step 2: Get a Quote

    Navigate to our "Parts" page and paste the link into the form. We will review the item and send you a complete, all-inclusive quote covering the item cost, our fee, and shipping to your location.

  3. Step 3: Payment

    Once you approve the quote, we will send you an invoice. We accept payment via bank transfer or credit card. After payment is confirmed, we will secure the item on your behalf.

  4. Step 4: Shipping & Delivery

    We handle all domestic logistics within Japan, package your item securely, and manage international shipping and customs paperwork. You will receive tracking information as soon as it's available.
